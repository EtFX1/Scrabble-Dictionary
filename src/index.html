Honest

/ˈɒnɪst/

Definition

adjective

(of a person or institution) Scrupulous with regard to telling the truth; not given to swindling, lying, or fraud; upright.

"I haven't been totally honest with you"

Synonyms

  • Outspoken
  • Truthful
  • Reliable
  • Conscientious
  • Genuine

Antonyms

  • Dishonest
  • Lying
  • Untruthful
  • False
  • Mendacious
